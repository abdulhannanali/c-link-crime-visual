How Safe is SoDo?

Seattle's SoDo (South of Downtown) neighborhood gets a lot of people passing through. Average attendance for a Seahawks home game at Century Link Field was 68,412 during the 2014-2015 season, accoring to Statista, and that doesn't take into account attendance at Mariners games, Sounders games, or the various concerts and events that take place at SoDo's stadiums. With such a high concentration of people, especially during events where emotions can run high and alcohol is flowing, there's bound to be some influence on crime rates.
